About Me

I spent the first eight years of my professional career in the Cargo Insurance industry, but after my job was affected by the COVID-19 pandemic I decided to go back to school. I enrolled in a program to learn web development, a skill that had piqued my interest for years. While I still draw on my experience with insurance and cargo transportation, I have come to appreciate the versatility that coding provides. It is certainly a skill that is sought after in many industries, and I'm excited to dive into my next venture wherever it might be.
